Welcome to SVDC

SV Degree & PG College has a tryst with excellence. We strive for excellence by ensuring a trinity of superlatives-students, faculty and infrastructure; cohabit in a congenial and conducive environment. We aspire to develop professionals with sound knowledge and skill, respect for profession, social and national values and ethics.
